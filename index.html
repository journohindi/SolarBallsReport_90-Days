Problems Found - Long Videos

1. The Episodic Death Spiral +

Forcing narratives into 3-part series kills CTR. "Suraj Tak Ka Mission" bled from 90K (Part 1) to 54K (Part 3). YouTube evaluates each video individually; a non-subscriber seeing "Part 3" on their homepage will not click. This fractures the audience and halts algorithmic velocity.

2. Dead-Weight Subscriber Base +

Having 875K subscribers but pulling 45K views sends a catastrophic signal to the algorithm. When YouTube pushes a new upload to the core audience and 95% ignore it, the system immediately categorizes the video as low-quality and ceases broader distribution.

3. Severe Metadata Inefficiency +

Mobile SEO character limits cut off titles at ~50 characters. Current titles waste this prime real estate on English text, duplicate phrasing, and structural tags (e.g., "Our Planets Gone Rogue Part 2 || Hindi..."). The translation is too literal and formal, failing to capture the click-intent of the grassroots Hindi demographic.

Problems Found - Shorts Pipeline

1. Zero Organic Funnel +

Shorts production has been abandoned. You have effectively severed the highest-converting top-of-funnel discovery mechanism on the platform. The only metrics generating right now are from legacy uploads.

2. Asset Mismanagement +

The channel produces high-quality, re-watchable 3D animation for long-form, but fails to extract the high-octane 30-second segments for vertical formats. This is a gross mismanagement of existing IP.

Strategic Restructure Protocol

Long-Form Restructure

  • Terminate Episodic Framing: Consolidate episodes into single 15-20 minute features to boost AVD. If splits are mandatory, title them as completely independent events. Remove "Part 1/2" identifiers.
  • Aam Bolchal Metadata Optimization: Strip out English prefixes and formal translations. Exploit the first 50 characters with conversational Hindustani stakes. (e.g., Instead of "Lost Earth Part 2", use "Earth ki nayi jagah kahan hai? 😱")
  • Hypothetical Pivot: Data proves standalone "What If" scenarios out-perform lore videos by 500%+. Reallocate 80% of production strictly to high-concept hypotheticals (e.g., "Agar Suraj Gayab Ho Gaya").

Shorts Funnel Activation

  • Mandatory Extraction Pipeline: Enforce a strict 1:3 ratio. Every long-form video must yield three 45-second, vertically re-framed vertical shorts.
  • Hard Cross-Linking: Utilize the YouTube Studio "Related Video" functionality on every Short to create a direct algorithmic bridge to the source long-form video.
